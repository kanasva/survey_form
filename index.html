Survey Form for changing the roof deck to be more functional

The roof deck of our flat has enormous space. However, 1/5 of the area has been used just for storing water tanks, and another 4/5 empty space is just for getting the sunshine.

Not many years ago, one of our residents suggested improving the uses of the roof deck. Nevertheless, it was controversial due to many concerns about the leak of water to the floor below; noise that can disturb residents, especially on the high floors.

Anyway, for this year of Covid-19, people value more about public spaces. And there are many solutions to cure those problems. Thus, it is time to reconsider changing the roof deck to be more functional!

Please fill this form to vote. If more than a half of ownership votes AGREE, we will have more enjoyable living!
